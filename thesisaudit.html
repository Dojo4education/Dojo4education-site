AI oral defense trainer

Knowing is one thing.
Explaining it aloud is another.

ThesisAudit simulates an academic defense: it asks questions based on your document, accepts voice answers, and shows where your reasoning becomes weak before it happens in front of a committee.

A text may feel clear while reading. But when you try to answer aloud, gaps appear in logic, terminology, and argumentation. ThesisAudit helps find them early.

// how it works

A structured rehearsal, not just random questions

01
Upload your material
Thesis, course paper, report, lecture notes, textbook chapters — any preparation document in .docx format.
02
Receive questions
AI analyzes the material and generates questions about the topic, methodology, conclusions, practical value, and key terms.
03
Answer by voice
Answer aloud as you would during a real defense. Voice answers are transcribed and analyzed.
04
Follow-up questions
Based on your answers, the system asks additional questions to test depth of understanding and argument logic.
05
Get the final report
A detailed report with readiness level, strengths, weak points, and specific areas to improve before the real defense.

ThesisAudit

AI trainer for thesis and course defense

ThesisAudit is a desktop app that simulates an examination board. It asks questions about your paper, accepts voice answers, analyzes them and offers follow-ups. You receive a report with strengths and weaknesses.

How it works

  • 1. Upload a .docx file — thesis, course paper or essay.
  • 2. Get 10 questions about its content.
  • 3. Answer by voice as if at the defense.
  • 4. The system analyzes answers and forms clarifying questions.
  • 5. Receive a final report with argument analysis.

Credits and terms

After sign-in you get 5 free credits to try.
1 credit = 5000 words (rounded up).
Top up via Telegram Stars (packages of 200 and 1000 stars).
Credits are active for 1 month, then freeze until the next donation.
